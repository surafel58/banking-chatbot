# Branch Hours and Locations

## General Branch Hours

Most SecureBank branches operate on the following schedule:

- **Monday - Friday**: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- **Saturday**: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- **Sunday**: Closed

## Holiday Hours

All branches are closed on the following holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Columbus Day
- Veterans Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

## Extended Hours Locations

Select branches offer extended hours until 7:00 PM on weekdays. Please check our branch locator or contact your local branch to confirm specific hours.

## ATM Availability

Our ATMs are available 24/7 for your convenience at all branch locations and select retail partners.
