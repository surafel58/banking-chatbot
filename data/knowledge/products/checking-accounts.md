# Checking Account Options

## Basic Checking

Perfect for everyday banking needs.

### Features
- No minimum balance requirement
- Free online and mobile banking
- Free debit card
- Unlimited check writing
- Access to 50,000+ fee-free ATMs nationwide

### Fees
- Monthly maintenance fee: $10 (waived with $500 minimum daily balance or $250 monthly direct deposit)
- Overdraft fee: $35 per item
- Out-of-network ATM: $3

## Premium Checking

Enhanced features for active banking customers.

### Features
- All Basic Checking features
- No monthly maintenance fee
- No overdraft fees (up to $50 overdraft protection)
- Preferred rewards program (1% cash back on debit purchases)
- Free checks
- Identity theft protection
- Travel and shopping benefits

### Requirements
- $1,500 minimum daily balance OR
- $3,000 in combined deposit and investment balances

## Student Checking

Designed for students under 25.

### Features
- No monthly fees while enrolled in school
- No minimum balance
- Free online and mobile banking
- Free debit card
- Financial education resources
- Automatic upgrade to Basic Checking at age 25

### Requirements
- Valid student ID
- Must be between 16-24 years old

## Business Checking

For small business owners and entrepreneurs.

### Features
- 200 free transactions per month
- Business debit card
- Online business banking
- Mobile deposit
- Payment processing integration

### Fees
- Monthly fee: $15 (waived with $5,000 minimum balance)
- Additional transactions: $0.50 each

## Opening an Account

- **Online**: Apply in 10 minutes at securebank.com
- **In Branch**: Visit any location with two forms of ID
- **Mobile**: Use our mobile app to apply
- **Minimum Opening Deposit**: $25 for most accounts
