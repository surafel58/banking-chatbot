# Lost or Stolen Card Policy

## Immediate Actions

If your credit or debit card is lost or stolen:

1. **Report Immediately**: Contact us 24/7 at (555) 123-4567 or through our mobile app
2. **Card Freeze**: Your card will be frozen immediately to prevent unauthorized use
3. **Transaction Review**: We'll review recent transactions for any suspicious activity
4. **Fraud Protection**: You're protected under our Zero Liability policy

## Zero Liability Protection

- **No liability** for unauthorized transactions when you report promptly
- Must report within **60 days** of the statement date
- Does not apply to PIN-based transactions if PIN was compromised through negligence

## Replacement Card Options

### Standard Delivery (Free)
- Arrives in 5-7 business days
- Shipped to your registered mailing address
- No additional fees

### Express Delivery ($25)
- Arrives in 1-2 business days
- Available for urgent situations
- Fee waived for premium account holders

### Virtual Card (Immediate)
- Available instantly in our mobile app
- Use for online purchases immediately
- Free for all customers

## Temporary Measures

While waiting for your replacement card:
- Use your virtual card for online purchases
- Access cash at any branch with valid ID
- Set up mobile wallet for contactless payments

## Security Tips

To prevent card loss or theft:
- Never share your PIN or card details
- Sign your card immediately upon receipt
- Keep card in a secure location
- Enable transaction alerts in the mobile app
- Review statements regularly
